A design for a living space inside the bunker at Oostende, for fashion designer Gareth Pughen and photographer Matthew Stone.

The concept starts from their work ethic. In their early years, they squatted a warehouse, in wich they lived worked with a group of friends. (A court order ultimately forced them to leave the building.) They say that their current studio is unheated and has only two electrical outlets.

The design is characterized by raw aesthetics. This is achieved by retaining the character of the original building. The weathered concrete walls emerge stronger through the use of smooth- finished materials such as marble and alabaster. The light coming from above creates an ethereal atmosphere. The raw aesthetics makes their pure spirit remain.
